      Sysdig Integrations

      Metrics can be integrated with Sysdig Monitor from a number of platforms, including Kubernetes (described in the Installation instructions), JMX, StatsD, Prometheus, and from a wide range of applications. Many metrics are collected by default out of the box; you can also extend the integration or create custom metrics.
